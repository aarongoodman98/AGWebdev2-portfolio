Facebook For Education

Again creating groups can be helpful for entire classes or small study groups, this allows for easy sharing of information and communication, this can be done without students having to friend eachother for what ever reason. Scheduling events is easy by just creating an event page for a project deadline or test date, where the students can be reminded when work is due. Facebook messenger can be used to make teachers aware of student absences and also by teachers.

Facebook For Education

When a class or lecture is over you can upload all the notes and slides from the class to your private facebook group or private messenger group so your students can open it at any time after class. By having these group pages students can give their thoughts on what they found helpful and what they didn't. They can also give the teacher ideas on what they can do to make the class more enjoyable and engaging. Teachers and students can use facebook to promote college events and trips regarding their subject or course.
