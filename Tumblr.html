Tumblr For Education

First, what is Tumblr??, Tumblr is an American microblogging and social media website founded in 2005. Tumblr allows anyone post muiltimedia and other types of content for a short-term blog post. Anyone can follow others users blogs, all bloggers can create their own private blog posts for people to see. Posts can consist of photos, videos, links, music and videos. Tumblr's deomographic is a young age group.

Creating A Tumblr Blog

Creating a page on tumblr is very similar to any other social networking site. Future users sign up for an account by entering their name and email address, and making a password, very simple!!!. You can custom design your page, inspired by the topic or subject or choose from a selection of pre existing templates. The created blog works as a content posting tool for almost all mediums such as text, links and videos.
