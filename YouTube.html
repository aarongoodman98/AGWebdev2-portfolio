How To Use YouTube:

Before I get into the basics of YouTube, what is YouTube???. Well, YouTube is an online video sharing platform based in San Bruno, California. YouTube was created by three past PayPal employees who sold the website to Google for 1.65 billion U.S dollars. Since then the way YouTube operates has completely changed, and is at the fore-front of online content.

YouTube is a very basic platform, very straight foward. Before you can use the website or app, you have to create a google email account. This is done simply by going onto google.com, click "gmail" in the top corner, enter your details and thats it. Then you simply go to YouTube enter your gmail account details and you go from there. If you don't want an account you can go straight to YouTube.com and start watching put you won't be able to complete normal functions.

YouTube consists of three main elements, the homepage, the trending page and your personal subscription page.

YouTube In Education

YouTube is one of the best social media tools that is also an amazing educational tool. YouTube allows its users to post videos of almost anything possible, from tutorial videos, to prank videos. People of all professions post videos related to the area of work. Informative videos are posted by Lawyers, doctors, personal trainers and teachers. YouTube has an enormous catalogue of teachers giving video tutorials/lessons on their subject, you can find classes from maths to music, and everything else in between.

YouTube allows anyone who wants to post content post it, all different genres all ages all around the world.

The majority of poeple that visit YouTube are looking for some form of entertainment but a large group of people look to YouTube for help, weither that be in life, mental health of for learning/educational purposes.
