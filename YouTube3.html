YouTube For Education

Along with YouTube Learning and YouTube Teachers, Google who own YouTube also provide educational content online, through YouTube. Their YouTube channel is called "Google For Education", this YouTube page is a vry active one with videos being posted weekly. Google for Education provides learning for everyone, anywhere. They believe that every student and every teacher, in all classrooms, deserves the tools and skills that set them up for success in lifeand the future they want to build for themselves.So they're committed to supporting students, by working with educators, building a strong community online and making impactful investments that help people get access to education through technology.

Google Classrooms

Google who own YouTube also have their own online learning network called "Google classroms",google classroom is a free based web service developed by google for schools. The aim of google classroom is to create, deliver, distribute and grade homework and projects without the use of pages or notebooks. It speeds up the process of getting the finished work from student directly to the teachers.
