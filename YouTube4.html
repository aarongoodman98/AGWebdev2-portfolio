YouTube For Education

Overall YouTube has everything you need to know, about anything!!!, Now I'm going to list why YouTube is essential in modern education, and why YOU as a educator need to implement this into your teaching practices. In class you can show students news clips and make everyone voice their opinion on the story to engage the minds and develop their own opinion, that will generate topical conversations. Educational videos are available on the platform that are short, funny and engage the brain. When you are studying english, videos of poets reading their work can be shown. Educational documentries produced by the likes of BBC are uploaded online in small clips.

Reasons To Use YouTube In The Classroom

Use YouTubeEDU for videos made for educational purposes. Teachers themselves can create videos for their students on what they need to know for tests or for the students that were sick on that day. Videos can be created for certain school groups and shared via a private link. Teaching some students through video content can be more effective than reading or writing depending on the kids ability. YouTube is at our finger tips, we can all use it to get better at certain things, along with teaching others through video, which for some is the best and easiest way to learn.
