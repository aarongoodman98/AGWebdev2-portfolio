Being Safe On Instagram

Social media and college/school are very different so you have to know your boundries when it comes to knowing what to post. As a teacher you should be aware of your own college policies. Such as are you allowed to post pictures of your students online, can you use your college name in posts, does the school administration need to have access to the account details, and to what extent can you interact with your students on such platforms.

Instagram As A Tool

Whether you are teaching an arts, media or sports/exercise degree social media is an unlimited tool of resources, just as an example. If your teaching art, instead of showing art in a slide show or power point open up an artists instagram page, or online portfolio. If you are teaching exercise movemnets to a class, show the students nstagram accounts run by trained P.T's, or physiothreapists demonstrating certain moves and methods. Media students can learn by watching photographers and videographers posts, and editing skills. Exploring a certain hashtag related to the subject will enable you to find many examples of people working in the industry their aspiring to work in.

Setting up a course or classroom instagram doesn't have to be a difficult thing, you'll find it takes very little time, and you will be able to engage with your whole student body quicker.
