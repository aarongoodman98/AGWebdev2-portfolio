Instagram For Education

As teachers you should you Instagram to remind the students of the fun their having or had in class, during lunch or even on a school or college trip. Instagram is a part of most students lives in this generation, so use it when it comes to lesson planning. Just like other social media platforms on Instagram you can post your students homework, project assignments, you can even psot video and picture tutorials of the homework or practical work that will appear in their instagram feed.

Another example of using instagram as a teacher is this, imagine you are an english teacher, get your students to post pictures of the book their reading at the moment or their favourite book they've read and use a hastag for your class, then all you do is search that tag on instagram and all your students book posts will appear.
