How Teachers Can Use Tumblr

Tumblr can be used as a direct web page between you and your students, you can store your pdfs', word docs, graphs, etc... As a teacher you would'nt have to stress about printing pages and pages of copies, everything could be on your private blog. If you come up iwth ideas or question you can upload it straight to tumblr and your students can reply in the blog post. Teachers have utilzed Tumblr to help eachother, there are several teacher based Tumblr blogs on the website, full of ideas, information and guidance.

Involving Students In Tumblr

You can use Tumblr to expand yout students creativty, for example if you are an english teacher start giving your students videos to watch on literature, poetry, spoken word, link them websites on the topics your covering at the moment instead of pages of reading in the classroom and at home. On Tumblr you have no limit with the use of digital media, coming up with new ideas for your class Tumblr will also text your creativity as a teacher which can be good for your students.
