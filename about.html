Social Media For Education

The table above shows a list of the five socail media tools/apps that I have decided are the most suitable to use in modern education. YouTube is probably the most popular video sharing platform on the internet and has been for the last decade. Reddit is a group forum website where people join online groups that are of interest to them and ask and answer questions. Facebook is social media website used to connect with people from all over the world, by posting pictures and status updates. Tumblr is a blogging website which was very popular in the early 2010-2012, since then people have left traditonal blogging and moved to video or audio content. Instagram is an extremely popular photo and video sharing app that provides all types of content imaginable.
