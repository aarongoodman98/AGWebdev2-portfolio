How Teachers Can Use Tumblr

After making a blog on Tumblr you can set up multiple sections for assignments, the syllabus and more, such as reminders and test dates. Your students can consume the content through any web browser and you the teacher can deliver all the material in one place. All teachers can use Tumblr as a resource sharing tool for other teachers to, by giving people tips and tricks to work smarter and faster.

Tumblr For Teachers

For teachers perosnal use their are many blogs about teachers, wrtten by teachers giving help and information that they find useful, and their perspective as a teacher in different education systems. A popular teacher blog is "eSchool", which is a blog on different ways to manage your school and class systems to mange your daily work load and keep track of evrything neccesary for you to work to your best ability.
